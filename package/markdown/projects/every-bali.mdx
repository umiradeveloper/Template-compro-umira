This project commenced on May 2025 and is developed on a total land area of 13,736 sqm, with a total building area of 26,550 sqm. Executed by Umira Global under a structured design and build approach, the scope of work includes architectural design, structural works, MEP systems, and on-site construction, all carried out in accordance with safety standards and technical specifications.

Throughout the construction process, the project is managed with a focus on quality control, timeline efficiency, and material accuracy to ensure optimal performance and durability. By integrating professional project management and experienced technical teams, Umira Global aims to deliver a well-executed project that meets functional requirements and long-term value expectations.
