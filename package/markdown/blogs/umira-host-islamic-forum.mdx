Umira Group recently hosted a community Islamic forum at Pendopo Omah Umira, welcoming residents from the surrounding neighborhood. The event created a warm and engaging atmosphere, bringing families together to learn and reflect.

#### Key Insights Delivered by the Speaker

The forum was led by Ustadz Ahmad Anshori, Lc., M.Pd., who shared clear and meaningful reminders about the importance of seeking knowledge and continuing to learn in daily life. He emphasized values that support personal growth, strong character, and a deeper understanding of Islamic teachings. Participants listened attentively throughout the session. 

#### Community Engagement and Atmosphere

The gathering was filled with meaningful discussions and a strong sense of togetherness. Residents from different age groups attended in person, while others joined through the online Zoom livestream. This allowed more people to benefit from the event. The venue remained lively yet well-organized, reflecting Umira Group’s commitment to providing accessible community programs.

#### Appreciation and Future Hope

Umira Group is grateful for the strong participation and positive response from the community. The event strengthened the bond between Umira and local residents, and we hope this forum will continue to inspire many others. We look forward to hosting more uplifting and educational activities in the future.
